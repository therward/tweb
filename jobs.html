The Disney College Program

During my time in the Disney College Program, I had the opportunity to work as a Cast Member at The Seas with Nemo & Friends attraction in Walt Disney World Resort, Orlando, Florida. My responsibilities included ensuring smooth operation of the ride and hosting the Turtle Talk with Crush interactive show, while providing exceptional guest experiences. Throughout my program, I consistently applied Disney's Five Keys: Safety, Courtesy, Inclusion, Show, and Efficiency to maintain a high standard of service and create a magical atmosphere for visitors. This valuable experience allowed me to develop my customer service and teamwork skills, while gaining a deeper understanding of the inner workings of a world-renowned entertainment company.
